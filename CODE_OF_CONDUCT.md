# Code of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, respectful, and specific.
- Assume technical disagreement is normal; keep it focused on the work.
- Give reproducible examples when reporting problems.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping threads on topic.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic, hostile, or bad-faith participation.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content; warn participants; close threads; or block accounts when behavior harms the project or community.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. If the concern is also security-sensitive, use the security reporting process instead of a public issue.
